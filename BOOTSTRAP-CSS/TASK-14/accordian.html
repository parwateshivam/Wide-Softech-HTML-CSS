The capital of India is New Delhi. It serves as the seat of the Indian government and is home to key institutions like the Supreme Court, Parliament, and Rashtrapati Bhavan. New Delhi is part of the larger National Capital Territory of Delhi, which includes other surrounding districts and urban areas.

The capital of Maharashtra is Mumbai, which also serves as the financial and commercial hub of India. It is the most populous city in India and is known for its bustling economy, entertainment industry, and iconic landmarks. While Mumbai is the primary capital, Nagpur is recognized as the second capital, particularly during the winter months, as per the Nagpur Pact.
